Do I make a registeration for an account?

Even if you do not have an account, you can read the stories. But you will need to login with an account when you want to do many other options in Guriba. You can register for an account here.

Can I read stories offline (airplane mode)?

We're so sorry about that. Offline mode cannot get at Guriba website. But we're planning to develop Guriba application. At that time, you will be able the offline mode. Untill now, stories can be read only with online mode.

What makes Guriba different?

We offers the needs of our writers and readers by providing you with various features. We always wait for your suggestions and feedback. When you give suggesions, we will review our features with the result of your suggestions. You can give your suggestions here.

How do I add new tags to my story?

Before upload your story, you will be able to add and delete tags from there. After uploading, you will not be able to edit tags.

Can I delete my account permanently?

You can edit, update or correct your personal information at your profile. But you can do deleting your account yourself. You can request to us for delete here.

Can I write translation stories?

Of Course, you can. But you have to give credit to original writer. If not, you will be in problem when original writer saw your story.
